# Identifier System  
Astitva Knowledge Archive

This document defines the identifier conventions used across the Astitva archive.

---

## Series Prefixes

SVS — Svādhyāya Saṁhitā  
SGB — Svagarbhā Series  
VHC — Vedic Hṛdaya Cakṣu

---

## Numbering Format

SVS notes:

SVS-0001  
SVS-0002  
SVS-0003

Four-digit numbering is used to allow large expansion.

---

SGB monographs:

SGB-001  
SGB-002

---

VHC works:

VHC-001  
VHC-002

---

## Stability Rule

Once assigned, an identifier must never change.

Identifiers remain permanent even if a work is revised.

---

## Citation Format

Example citation:

SVS-0003  
Astitva Archive, Svādhyāya Saṁhitā.
