## Reflection

Svādhyāya is not merely study but an inward discipline of reflective engagement with revealed and transmitted knowledge.  
It combines attentive reading, contemplation, and intellectual reconstruction.

Through repeated engagement with foundational texts, svādhyāya becomes a method of uncovering conceptual structures embedded within the tradition.
